Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kanji Boss. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, please contact us.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) directly through the Kanji Boss app. The app is designed to function as a dictionary and learning tool without requiring user registration.

3. Google Analytics for Firebase

Our app uses Google Analytics for Firebase to help us understand how users interact with our application. This service collects data such as:

  • Number of users and sessions
  • Session duration
  • Operating systems and device models
  • In-app events and geography

This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the app experience.

4. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can manage these preferences through your device settings.

5. Data Retention

All learning progress is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data on our servers.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to our support team.
