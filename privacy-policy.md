# Privacy Policy for Neuro Trivia

**Last Updated:** April 26, 2026

Welcome to **Neuro Trivia**, an application developed by **Soranix**. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide to Us
We may collect information you provide directly to us when you use the App, such as when you interact with the trivia features, respond to questions, or adjust app settings. Because the App uses artificial intelligence (AI) to generate or evaluate trivia, any text or preferences you input may be processed by these services.

### B. Automatically Collected Information
When you access the App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:
*   **Device Information:** Your mobile device ID (such as Apple IDFA or Google AAID), device model, operating system version, and device settings.
*   **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the App, including crash reports and performance data.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
*   Provide, operate, and maintain the App.
*   Generate personalized trivia content using third-party AI services.
*   Improve, personalize, and expand the App's features.
*   Understand and analyze how you use the App.
*   Serve relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.
*   Find and prevent fraud and fix bugs/crashes.

## 3. Third-Party Services and APIs

The App utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. The primary third-party service providers we use include:

*   **Google AdMob:** We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID) and cookies to serve ads based on your past usage.
*   **Firebase:** We use Firebase for app analytics, crash reporting, and potentially database management to improve app stability and performance.
*   **OpenAI / Google Generative AI:** We use advanced AI models to power our trivia generation and checking. Any data sent to these APIs is subject to their respective privacy and data usage policies. We do not use your personal information to train these models.

For more information on how these third parties handle your data, please refer to their respective Privacy Policies:
*   [Google Privacy Policy (AdMob & Firebase)](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
*   [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)

## 4. Local Storage

We use local storage (such as React Native Async Storage) on your device to save your progress, scores, and user preferences locally. This data generally remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless connected to a cloud-save feature.

## 5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or higher, depending on the jurisdiction). We do not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13. If you become aware of any data we have collected from children under age 13, please contact us immediately so we can take steps to remove it.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates.
